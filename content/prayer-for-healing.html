Prayer for Healing with Bible Verses

Many users search “prayer for healing bible verse.” Use this prayer as a starting point:

Lord Jesus, You are near to the brokenhearted. Please heal my body, calm my mind, and restore my hope. Give wisdom to doctors, patience to my family, and peace to my soul. In Your name, amen.

Healing Scriptures

  • James 5:14–15
  • Psalm 147:3
  • Jeremiah 17:14
