Prayer for Family and Marriage Restoration

A Bible-based prayer for marriage and family peace, with practical reconciliation steps rooted in grace and truth.

Focus keyword: prayer for family

Key Bible Passages

  • Colossians 3:13 — Forgive as the Lord forgave you.
  • Ephesians 4:2-3 — Be completely humble and gentle; be patient, bearing with one another in love.
  • Joshua 24:15 — As for me and my house, we will serve the Lord.

Practical Faith Steps

  1. Pray together for 5 minutes daily.
  2. Practice one honest conversation with no blame language.
  3. Choose forgiveness as a process, not a feeling.
