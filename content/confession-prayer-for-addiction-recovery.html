Confession Prayer for Addiction Recovery

A compassionate confession prayer for addiction recovery: repentance, accountability, and daily surrender to Jesus.

Focus keyword: confession prayer for healing

Key Bible Passages

  • James 5:16 — Confess your sins to each other and pray for each other so that you may be healed.
  • 1 Corinthians 10:13 — God is faithful; he will provide a way out so that you can endure it.
  • Romans 8:1 — There is now no condemnation for those who are in Christ Jesus.

Practical Faith Steps

  1. Confess specifically, not vaguely.
  2. Invite one mature believer into accountability.
  3. Build replacement habits that move you toward life.
