Bible Verses About Faith and Trust in God

If you are searching for Bible verses about faith, this guide gives practical, everyday ways to trust God when outcomes are unclear.

Focus keyword: bible verses about faith

Key Bible Passages

  • Hebrews 11:1 — Faith is confidence in what we hope for and assurance about what we do not see.
  • Proverbs 3:5-6 — Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding.
  • Mark 9:23 — Everything is possible for one who believes.

Practical Faith Steps

  1. Name one area where you are over-controlling.
  2. Surrender it to God in prayer using Proverbs 3:5-6.
  3. Take one obedient action today even before feelings change.
