Jesus on Love and Forgiveness

If you searched for “Jesus quotes about love and forgiveness,” these themes matter most:

  • Love your neighbor as yourself (Mark 12:31)
  • Forgive seventy times seven (Matthew 18:22)
  • Blessed are the peacemakers (Matthew 5:9)

Relationship Reset Framework

  1. Tell the truth in love.
  2. Set healthy boundaries without hatred.
  3. Choose forgiveness even when trust rebuilds slowly.
