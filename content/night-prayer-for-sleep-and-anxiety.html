Night Prayer for Sleep and Anxiety

If your mind races at night, use this Christian night prayer and calming verses to rest in God’s care.

Focus keyword: night prayer for sleep

Key Bible Passages

  • Psalm 4:8 — In peace I will lie down and sleep, for you alone, Lord, make me dwell in safety.
  • Matthew 11:28 — Come to me, all you who are weary and burdened, and I will give you rest.
  • Psalm 23:1-2 — He makes me lie down in green pastures; he leads me beside quiet waters.

Practical Faith Steps

  1. Turn off bright screens 30 minutes before bed.
  2. Write down your top worries and hand them to God in prayer.
  3. Recite Psalm 4:8 before sleeping.
