Prayer for Depression and Hopelessness

You are not abandoned. This prayer for depression combines biblical truth and compassionate next steps when you feel numb or exhausted.

Focus keyword: prayer for depression

Key Bible Passages

  • Psalm 42:11 — Why, my soul, are you downcast? Put your hope in God.
  • Romans 15:13 — May the God of hope fill you with all joy and peace as you trust in him.
  • Lamentations 3:21-23 — Because of the Lord’s great love we are not consumed; his mercies are new every morning.

Practical Faith Steps

  1. Tell God exactly how you feel without editing.
  2. Reach out to one trusted person today.
  3. Use one verse as your anchor thought when emotions spiral.
