Daily Devotional: Finding Purpose in Ordinary Life

A short daily devotional on purpose: how small faithful actions become meaningful calling over time.

Focus keyword: daily devotional purpose

Key Bible Passages

  • Micah 6:8 — Act justly, love mercy, walk humbly with your God.
  • Colossians 3:23 — Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord.
  • Ephesians 2:10 — We are God’s workmanship, created in Christ Jesus to do good works.

Practical Faith Steps

  1. Do one hidden act of service today.
  2. Work with excellence even when no one applauds.
  3. Ask God to align ambition with love.
