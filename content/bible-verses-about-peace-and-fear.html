Bible Verses About Peace and Fear

Need peace now? These Bible verses about fear and peace show how Jesus meets anxiety, panic, and overthinking with steady presence.

Focus keyword: bible verses about peace

Key Bible Passages

  • John 14:27 — Peace I leave with you; my peace I give you.
  • Psalm 34:4 — I sought the Lord, and he answered me; he delivered me from all my fears.
  • 2 Timothy 1:7 — God gave us not a spirit of fear but of power, love, and self-discipline.

Practical Faith Steps

  1. Pause and breathe slowly for 60 seconds.
  2. Read John 14:27 twice.
  3. Ask: what is one faithful next step I can take right now?
