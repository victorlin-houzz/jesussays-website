Daily Devotional: Forgiveness When You Still Feel Hurt

Forgiveness is rarely instant. This devotional helps you forgive biblically while maintaining wisdom and boundaries.

Focus keyword: daily devotional forgiveness

Key Bible Passages

  • Matthew 6:14 — If you forgive other people, your heavenly Father will also forgive you.
  • Romans 12:18 — If it is possible, as far as it depends on you, live at peace with everyone.
  • Luke 23:34 — Father, forgive them, for they do not know what they are doing.

Practical Faith Steps

  1. Name the wound honestly.
  2. Release revenge to God in prayer.
  3. Choose one healthy boundary moving forward.
