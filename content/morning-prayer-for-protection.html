Start your day with a biblical morning prayer for protection, clarity, and spiritual strength before work, family, and decisions.

Focus keyword: morning prayer for protection

Key Bible Passages

  • Psalm 91:1-2 — Whoever dwells in the shelter of the Most High will rest in the shadow of the Almighty.
  • Psalm 121:8 — The Lord will watch over your coming and going both now and forevermore.
  • Ephesians 6:11 — Put on the full armor of God.

Practical Faith Steps

  1. Pray before touching your phone.
  2. Commit your calendar and meetings to God.
  3. Ask for protection over mind, body, family, and purpose.
