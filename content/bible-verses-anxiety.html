Bible Verses About Anxiety and Worry

If you searched “bible verses for anxiety,” start here. These passages are commonly sought because they are direct, practical, and comforting.

Top Scriptures

  • Philippians 4:6–7 — Pray instead of panic; God’s peace guards your heart.
  • 1 Peter 5:7 — Cast your anxiety on Him because He cares for you.
  • Matthew 6:34 — Don’t borrow tomorrow’s worries.
  • Isaiah 41:10 — Do not fear, for I am with you.
  • Psalm 56:3 — When I am afraid, I put my trust in You.

Simple 3-Step Prayer

  1. Name the fear honestly before God.
  2. Replace fear with one verse above.
  3. Ask for today’s strength, not lifetime certainty.
