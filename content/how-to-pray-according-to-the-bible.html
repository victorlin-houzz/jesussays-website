How to Pray According to the Bible (Simple Guide)

Learn how to pray according to Scripture with a beginner-friendly framework: praise, confession, request, surrender, and thanksgiving.

Focus keyword: how to pray according to the bible

Key Bible Passages

  • Matthew 6:9-13 — Jesus teaches the Lord’s Prayer as a model.
  • Philippians 4:6 — In everything, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God.
  • 1 John 5:14 — If we ask anything according to his will, he hears us.

Practical Faith Steps

  1. Start with praise: who God is.
  2. Confess honestly: where you missed the mark.
  3. Ask specifically: what you need today.
  4. End with surrender: not my will, but Yours.
